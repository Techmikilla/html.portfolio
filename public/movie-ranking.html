My Top Three Favourite Movies of 2023

The Marsh Girl

TheMarsh girl is a thrilling and suspenseful movie with a mystery solving vibe. The story line is about an isolated girl that had a rough childhood growing up. she even had to fend for herself which made her independent. She had one friend and lover in the whole community who had to one day, leave for school abroad, She was heartbroken as he did not return when he said he would. That heartbreaking experience led her to fall for a guy with bad intentions of which she had to find out the hard way when she bumped into him and his fiancee. She got her heart broken again in a community where no one liked her and as if this was not enough, she was bullied and raped by the same guy who used her while being engaged to another. Karma as it may, had him meet his doom soon in an accident and the marsh girl was accused of taking his life. Will she lose or will she win this case?

Love Tactics 1

This movie is a comedy drama about two people of the opposite gender who feel they know all about love and their different genders. They both strike a bet with thier friends, one to make a guy fall in love, the other to prove that he cannot fall for a girl. Who is going to win this,Why not watch the movie to find out?

Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story

This is a short series of fiction inspired by facts. It depicts a royal marriage where a queen and king live in separate worlds. Regardless of their differences, they are both still very much in love and willing to support each other. I love this movie because it depicts the meaning of true love and partnership. It is a rollercoaster of emotions, but if you are a fan of hopeless romantics and love i strongly recommend watching this movie.
